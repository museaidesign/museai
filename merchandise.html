What is style consistency?

Style consistency means your images should share similar visual characteristics such as:

  • Color palette
  • Artistic technique
  • Composition style
  • Level of detail

This helps train the AI to accurately replicate your unique artistic vision.

  • Minimum 15 images in consistent style
  • Minimum resolution: 1024x1024 pixels
  • High-quality, clear images
  • No text overlays
